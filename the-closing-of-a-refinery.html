The Closing of a Refinery

The Closing of a Refinery

// Director // Experimental short documentary // 2023 // ESMAD //

A soul-searching interview to ChatGPT about sustainability, climate change and the future of humankind in the wake of the decommission of the largest oil refinery in Northern Portugal.

The Closing of a Refinery is an attempt to address the economic, environmental and social consequences of the green energy politics. The use of an AI text generative program, that parrots our human ideas, holds a mirror that highlights the sterile simplicity we often use in our discourse about climate change and sustainability. Instead of providing answers, this film aim’s to incite engagement with the existential challenge posed by our current climate crisis. Produced with Escola Superior de Media Artes e Design (ESMAD) as part of a master thesis in documentary film.
