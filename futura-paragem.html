Futura Paragem

Futura Paragem

// Tutor // Colective Film // 2022 // Bando à Parte //

Feeling the problems of an isolated life in the housing complex, its residents strive to keep alive the hope that one day a bus will pass by to take them to a future stop.

A colective film developed over the course of twelve workshops held during the months of February, March, and April 2022 at the social projects Empreendimento de Mataduços, Fermentões, Guimarães.
