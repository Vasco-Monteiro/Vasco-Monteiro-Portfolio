Kino-Montra

Kino-Montra

// Director // Short documentary // 2022 // ESMAD //

Curtas de Vila do Conde is one of the oldest and most well respected film festivals in Portugal. Over 30 years after it's inception, how does the small town where it takes place sees the festival? A shop window tells us about that relationship.

Short documentary made during the first year of a master degree in documentary film in collaboration with the Festival de Curtas de Vila do Conde.
