Bussolar

Bussolar

// Tutor // Colective film // 2024 // Bússola //

What is love? How many are we? Where do we come from? Where are we going? Do we choose to attack or to embrace? To leave or to stay? On the path of affection we dared to find ourselves. The compass pointed us in a direction. Bussolar? What is that?

This short docu-fiction film is an investigation/reflection film by Coletivo Bússola, developed over the course of 16 workshops and meetings held at Casa do Povo de Fermentões, in Guimarães, between March 2023 and December 2024.
